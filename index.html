What we're about

This meetup is more than just turning up and hearing about cool stuff in the world of AI. We are a community, and we will be working collectively on projects that can benefit the community, maybe turn into commercial ventures, or maybe even change the world!

Do come along to one of our Meetups. We are hopefully of inspiring you to roll your sleeves up and become part of our active, vibrant community of enthusiasts working on real-world problems that may find solutions in the incredible potential of AI technology.

We are entering the next wave of technological innovation, powered by AI, ML and cognitive science. Come along and be part of it!
